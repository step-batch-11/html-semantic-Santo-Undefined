Rooftop Garden Are Changing Cities

Author: Maria Lopez

Published: March 15, 2026

Introduction

Urban farming is transforming unused city spaces into productive green zones. Rooftop gardens are leading this change.

Why Rooftop Gardens Matter ?

Cities face food insecurity and environmental stress. Rooftop gardens reduce heat and improve air quality. Urban farms can reduce city temperatures by 2°C. Average yield per rooftop: 500 kg per year.

Real-World Example

In 2024, the SkyGrow Project launched in New York City.
